"I am an MRM Medical Director and a mother of two boys — and I believe in the dream too."

Hanna Jesionowska, MD founded Manhattan Reproductive Medicine in 2001 as a deliberate alternative to the impersonal care of large fertility hospitals. At MIRI, one doctor means one vision of care — from your first consultation through retrieval and the moment you see a heartbeat.

The doctor behind MIRI

"Every family's dream deserves a champion."
Hanna Jesionowska, MD is board-certified in Reproductive Endocrinology and Infertility. She has dedicated her career to providing personalized, single-provider fertility care — a rare model in today's landscape. Dr. Hanna personally performs every monitoring visit, retrieval, and embryo transfer, ensuring continuity and trust throughout your journey.

Patients travel from across the US and internationally to work with Dr. Hanna. Her approach combines cutting-edge reproductive science with deep compassion, ensuring that every patient feels seen, heard, and supported at every step.
